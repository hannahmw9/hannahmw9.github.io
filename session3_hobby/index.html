My Favorite Hobby is Cooking!

by Hannah Whitaker

I’ve always loved working with my hands. Whether it’s painting or building something for the house, making in general is something I’ve always been drawn to.

When I moved into my first apartment after graduate school, I was living alone and obviously needing to feed myself somehow! I bought my first cookbook and started experimenting. My first oven didn’t read the temperature properly, so I ended up with my fair share of botched meals (including a Thanksgiving Turkey). It wasn’t great at first, but I refined the skills and techniques needed to pull together a great meal.

Today, the cookbook collection is extensive, the meal complexity is much greater, and I find cooking to be a great escape once the busy workday is done. I love cooking for family and friends, tackling unknown recipes and skills, and even creating my own recipes based on my growing instinct.
